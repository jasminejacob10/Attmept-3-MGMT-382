What we do

Systems Assessment & Roadmapping

Current-state audit, data flows, and gap analysis mapped to business goals and KPIs.

  • Process mapping & swimlanes
  • Data model & integration points
  • Risk & controls (SOX-lite)

CRM Implementation

Configure pipelines, automate follow-ups, and unify customer data for sales and service.

  • Lead-to-cash workflow
  • Dashboards & pipeline velocity
  • Email/SMS automation

Supply Chain (SCM)

Inventory visibility, vendor portals, and demand planning to reduce stockouts & carrying costs.

  • Reorder points & safety stock
  • Supplier scorecards
  • EDI/API integrations

ERP Integrations

Modular ERP rollouts with clean master data, role security, and training.

  • Chart of accounts alignment
  • Procure-to-pay & order-to-cash
  • Reporting & audit trails

Collaboration & Knowledge

Intranet, file governance, and team hubs to reduce email overload and lost docs.

  • Versioning & permissions
  • Meeting notes → tasks
  • Searchable SOP library

Analytics & Dashboards

Operational dashboards tied to outcomes (cycle time, fill rate, CSAT, margin).

  • Data quality checks
  • Self-serve metrics
  • Forecast & alerts

* All implementations include testing, training, and simple documentation.
