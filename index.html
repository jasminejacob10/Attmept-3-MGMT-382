Our Values

  • Harmony: whole-person, culturally respectful care.
  • Peace: safe, transparent experiences and plain-language instructions.
  • Abundance: accessible options—mobile clinics, evening hours, and fair pricing.
  • Integrity: evidence-based practice and responsible data stewardship.

Services

Vaccination Appointments

Routine and travel immunizations with reminders and after-care notes.

  • Flu, COVID-19, Tdap, MMR, HPV, travel vaccines
  • Digital records and school/work documentation
  • On-site or mobile pop-up clinics

Annual Physicals

Head-to-toe exams focusing on prevention, lifestyle goals, and care plans.

  • Vitals, BMI, vision/hearing checks
  • Medication review and counseling
  • Personalized wellness goals

Health Screenings & Diagnostics

Fast, reliable tests with clear follow-up steps.

  • Blood pressure, A1C, cholesterol, rapid strep/flu
  • Cancer screenings coordination
  • Referrals and next-step guidance

How your visit works

  1. Book online or by phone.
  2. Visit your nearest Eloheh site or a mobile clinic.
  3. Get results and a simple follow-up plan.
